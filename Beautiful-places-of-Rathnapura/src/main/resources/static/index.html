Why Rathnapura is famous

“Rathnapura has absolutely everything” cited as the 2nd best place to visit in the world by National Geographic channel, Rathnapura is the best place to have your dream holiday, whatever your requirement is Rathnapura has it all. Whether you prefer to canoe rides on a dugout canoe in a inland lake or a little cove, drive or trek through cool plains in the hills, lush tropical evergreen forests, ancient monuments, temples and fortresses, colourful festivals and mingle with the ever smiling friendly people of Rathnapura welcoming you to their paradise.
